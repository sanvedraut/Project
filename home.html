About Us

D.Y. Patil Education Group is a renowned institution committed to providing high-quality education in various fields, including medicine, dentistry, engineering, management, and more. With a rich history of academic excellence and a focus on holistic development, we are a preferred choice for students seeking a bright future.

Our journey has been marked by significant achievements, including receiving an 'A' grade by NAAC in the previous two cycles (2009 'A' Grade with a 3.32 CGPA and 2014 'A' Grade with a 3.40 CGPA). We take pride in our recent re-accreditation by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with an 'A++' Grade.

In addition to NAAC, we have also achieved recognition from the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Health Care Providers (NABH) for our medical sciences division. D.Y. Patil Deemed to be University teaching hospital is the first to receive NABH accreditation in the country.

Furthermore, our commitment to quality extends to our laboratories, which have received accreditation from the National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL). NABL is a Constituent Board of Quality Council of India, and our labs meet the highest standards of quality and technical competence.
